Welcome to Latimore Life & Legacy

As an independent insurance agency rooted in Schuylkill Haven, Pennsylvania, we help individuals and families secure their financial futures. Our mission is to break generational cycles of financial vulnerability and build lasting legacies through life insurance, annuities and personalized planning.

Founded by Jackson Latimore, a first‑generation Pennsylvanian with deep ties to post‑industrial communities, our agency understands the unique challenges faced by everyday people in Schuylkill, Luzerne and Northumberland counties.
