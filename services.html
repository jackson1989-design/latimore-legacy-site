Services for Families

Our primary focus is helping individuals and families achieve lifetime income security and build lasting legacies. We take the time to learn about your goals, concerns and timeline before recommending any solutions.

Indexed Universal Life (IUL)

  • Provides lifelong coverage with cash-value growth linked to market indexes
  • Offers downside protection while allowing upside participation
  • Flexible premiums and death benefits to adapt as your needs change

Fixed & Variable Annuities

  • Guarantee steady income in retirement through fixed annuity payments
  • Participate in market growth with variable annuities, balancing risk and reward
  • Customize payout periods and beneficiary options

Term & Whole Life Insurance

  • Affordable term policies to protect your loved ones during key years
  • Whole life policies that build cash value you can access
  • Flexible riders for long-term care, critical illness and more

Legacy Planning

  • Estate liquidity strategies to help your beneficiaries avoid unnecessary taxes
  • Wealth transfer planning to ensure your assets reach the right people
  • Succession strategies for family businesses and farmland owners

Services for Business Owners

We also support small business owners with personal financial planning and protection strategies for their companies. As entrepreneurs ourselves, we know your livelihood and your business are tightly intertwined.

Key Person Insurance

  • Protect your company against the loss of a critical employee or founder
  • Provide financial stability during transitions or ownership changes
  • Customize coverage amounts to fit your company's size and revenue

Succession & Buy-Sell Planning

  • Create agreements that facilitate ownership transfer without conflict
  • Fund buy-sell agreements with life or disability insurance
  • Ensure business continuity for employees and clients

Retirement Benefits

  • Design retirement plans for yourself and your employees
  • Explore tax-advantaged options like SEP IRAs and Simple IRAs
  • Offer competitive benefits to attract and retain talent
